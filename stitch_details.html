Property Overview

Metropolitan Plaza is a Class A office building situated in the heart of New York's Financial District. Completed in 2018, this 42-story tower offers state-of-the-art office spaces with floor-to-ceiling windows, offering panoramic views of the Manhattan skyline and the East River.

The property features an award-winning lobby designed by leading architects, a 15,000 SF fitness center, and a rooftop garden terrace. With high-efficiency building systems and LEED Platinum certification, Metropolitan Plaza represents the pinnacle of modern workspace sustainability and employee wellness.
